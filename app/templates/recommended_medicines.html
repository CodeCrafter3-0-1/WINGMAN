Recommended Medicines (General Guide)

Signed in as {{ email }}

Informational only. Do not start, stop, or change medicines without clinician advice.

Heart / BP risk discussions

  • ACE inhibitors / ARBs (for blood pressure and kidney protection in selected patients)
  • Calcium channel blockers or thiazide diuretics for BP control plans
  • Statins for cholesterol and cardiovascular risk reduction

Diabetes risk discussions

  • Metformin is commonly used for type 2 diabetes and prediabetes in some cases
  • GLP-1 / SGLT2 class medicines may be considered in selected patients
  • A1c and glucose trends should guide treatment decisions

Respiratory risk discussions

  • Reliever inhalers (for example short-acting bronchodilators)
  • Controller inhalers (inhaled corticosteroid-based plans for asthma/COPD as indicated)
  • Vaccinations and trigger avoidance are important parts of care

Kidney and liver protection

  • Kidney-safe BP and diabetes control may lower long-term risk
  • Avoid frequent unsupervised painkiller use (especially some NSAIDs)
  • Alcohol reduction and weight management support liver health
