About Our Risk Model

HealthBot uses a transparent, rule-based scoring system that combines age, symptoms, medical history, lifestyle factors, and optional measurements. The model is calibrated for data completeness and designed for early screening support, not clinical diagnosis.

Important Safety Information

Responsible use and clinical safety guidelines

Not a Substitute for Care

This platform is a screening and support tool. It is not a substitute for clinical diagnosis, emergency care, or prescription decisions.

Data Quality Matters

Better input quality leads to better screening quality. Add optional measurements when available, but the system works with basic self-reported details.

Urgent Warning Signs

For severe chest pain, severe shortness of breath, sudden weakness, confusion, or fainting, seek urgent medical care immediately.
