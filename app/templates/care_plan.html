Recommended Care Plan and Tests

Signed in as {{ email }}

Vitals to track weekly

  • Blood pressure (target often around normal range unless your clinician sets otherwise)
  • Weight and waist trend
  • Activity minutes and sleep duration

Useful lab checks

  • Fasting glucose and HbA1c
  • Lipid profile (cholesterol panel)
  • Kidney panel (creatinine/eGFR, urine albumin)
  • Liver enzymes (ALT/AST) when risk is elevated

Lifestyle priorities

  • 150+ minutes/week moderate activity (as tolerated)
  • High-fiber meals, lower sugary drinks, lower excess salt
  • Smoking cessation and alcohol moderation

When to seek urgent care

  • Severe chest pain or severe shortness of breath
  • Sudden confusion, weakness, slurred speech, or fainting
  • Oxygen saturation persistently very low with symptoms
