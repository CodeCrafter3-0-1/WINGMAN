Need help understanding results?

Use these answers as a quick reference, then open chatbot or care plan for deeper guidance.

Is this a diagnosis tool?

No. It is a screening aid to highlight risk and suggest next steps.

Do I need all measurements?

No. Fasting glucose, SpO2, random glucose, and resting HR are optional. Add them only if available.

What if I see high or urgent risk?

Use the care plan and seek medical advice. For severe symptoms, seek urgent care immediately.

How to improve prediction quality?

Keep lifestyle and symptom data accurate, and update BP/measurement values regularly.
