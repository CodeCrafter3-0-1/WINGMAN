Use this program as a practical routine to improve risk factors shown on your dashboard.

Week 1: Baseline

  • Track blood pressure 3 times this week
  • Record sleep and activity daily
  • Reduce sugary drinks and processed snacks

Week 2: Build routine

  • Reach 120-150 active minutes this week
  • Add 1 high-fiber meal per day
  • Set a fixed sleep/wake schedule

Week 3: Intensify safely

  • Add 2 sessions of light strength training
  • Decrease salt and alcohol intake further
  • Review symptom changes

Week 4: Review and plan

  • Re-run your prediction in dashboard
  • Compare trends from week 1 to week 4
  • Share progress with a clinician if needed
